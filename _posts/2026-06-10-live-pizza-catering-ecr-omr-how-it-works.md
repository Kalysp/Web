A live pizza counter at an event is exactly what it sounds like — a pizza station, set up at your venue, where fresh pizzas are made and served to your guests in real time. It's one of those food experiences that people remember.

But if you haven't booked live catering before, the logistics can feel unclear. Here's how it actually works.

## Step 1: You Enquire

It starts with an enquiry — through our [Book Your Date form](/book-your-date/), by email, or on WhatsApp. We'll ask about:

- Your event date (or approximate timing if you're still planning)
- Your venue and location on ECR or OMR
- Expected number of guests / number of pizzas
- Which package you're interested in
- Any special requirements

We'll get back to you quickly — usually within 24 hours — to confirm availability and discuss the details.

## Step 2: We Discuss the Details

Once we're in touch, we'll confirm:

- Package and pricing
- Service time (2 hours for 20–40 pizzas, 3 hours for 41–70)
- Setup requirements at your venue (space, power, surface)
- Any add-ons — desserts, waiter service, styled setup

This is also where we talk through anything specific to your event — timings, dietary questions, and how you'd like the counter placed.

## Step 3: We Show Up and Set Up

On the day, our team arrives ahead of your event start time to set up the live pizza counter. This includes the oven, equipment, and all the prep work. You don't need to worry about any of it.

Setup time depends on the venue and setup type. We'll discuss this when you book.

## Step 4: Fresh Pizzas, Live

Once the event starts, so does the pizza. Guests come to the counter, pick their pizza, and watch it being made. The pizzas are served hot — straight from the oven.

For the Classic and Signature packages, we rotate through the varieties during the service window so everyone gets a chance to try each one.

## Step 5: We Pack Up

At the end of the service window, we pack up cleanly and efficiently. You don't have to deal with any of the equipment or cleanup on our side.

## What You Need to Provide

Not much. We handle everything on our end. You just need to:

- Confirm a space for the counter (we'll advise on size)
- Ensure power access at the venue
- Let us know any venue-specific rules or restrictions

We're based on the coastline and serve events from Adyar to Mahabalipuram. Beach houses, villas, corporate spaces, wedding venues, and private homes — we've catered at all of them along this stretch.
